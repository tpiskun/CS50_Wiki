I promise, these guys are funny:

After moving to Chicago, I decided to take a Stand Up class at The Second City training center to fuel my hunger and passion for Comedy. Here are some Comedians I discovered along the way:

  • Pete Davidson

    Pete Davidson has great storytelling skills which is what makes him a great Stand Up Comedian. He also sticks to his brand of being from Staten Island and Dark Humor.

  • Chris Redd

    My Stand-up teacher taught him. Really funny story about growing up in Chicago.

  • Sarah Silverman

    Love her sarcasm and dry humor.
